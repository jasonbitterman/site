Jason Bitterman, MD

Board Certified Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation Doctor

My goal is to help patients return to an active lifestyle and improve their quality of life.

I take a personalized approach, including guidance through a physical rehabilitation course, medications, lifestyle modifications for daily activity and exercise, and if indicated, injection therapies.

I specialize in diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal and nerve disorders, including neck pain, low back pain, spinal disc disorders, radiculopathy (sciatica), spinal stenosis, joint issues (such as hip, knee, and shoulder pain), tendinitis, neuropathies, and sports injuries.

I work at Hartford Healthcare and practice in the greater Hartford area.
